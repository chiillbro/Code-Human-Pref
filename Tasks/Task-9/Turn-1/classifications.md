## 6. Overall Preference Justification

I prefer Model B here. On the flip side, Model A does have the advantage of accepting `cst.Module` objects directly in its `apply_pep563_transform()` function rather than re-parsing strings like Model B does, and Model A's existing TYPE_CHECKING block reuse (appending to it rather than creating a new one) is a nice detail.
